Why am I here?

From an early age, I've had a strong passion about learning how or why something happens.

In today's world it seems like there are always new advances and I want to be part of an ecosystem That will always give me new challenges and allow me to show case my skills while still learning.

Where I am now?

Currently as a young and eager developer I am constantly reading new material on computer languages.

Exploring more ways of developing not just a website or an app, but a master piece.

I desire to hone my skills and take them to another level. To grow and get better is a passion of mine.

Where do I want to be?

Working with a team of like minded individuals who enjoy putting in as much effort as myself.

Being part of the reason why something not just looks good, but also works very well. It's a reward in itself to be able to achieve something more than another website.

I aim to be at a senior level and lead teams to achieve goals.
